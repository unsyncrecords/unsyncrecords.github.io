About

Mask off

We'll tell you the truth.

Why does Unsync exist?

It all began when i was working with another Label and got ripped off because i was not careful enough.

I gave up out of frustration and didn't do anything for a while..

After a couple of months, i have decided to get back up on my feet and try again.
But this time i would do it differently and make sure something like that mishap would NOT happen again.
Not to me and not to anyone that i work with.

Birth and Philosophy

So Unsync Records was born after 3 months of work and planning.

i have decided to create a platform that would NOT follow the standard Music Industry procedure, and focus solely on making money,
but rather focus on independence and creativity.

Unsync was never about chasing charts or pleasing algorithms. It's about keeping control over your own art, your own sound, your own timing.

Unsync Records does not pretend to be a polished corporate machine.
We are a workshop, a small room with medium-sized speakers, with a vision to do things differently.

Royalties and Cuts

We take 12% of royalties.
That's our share. No smoke, no random charges, no hidden tricks.

On top of that, the distributor takes a small cut too.
That's simply unavoidable, unfortunately..
We all have to eat and you get to eat the most.

Why take 12%?

Running a label costs time, energy and it needs to survive. But the main share will always stay with our Artists, where it belongs.

The Takeaway

We started Unsync Records to make music the way we want:

honest, independent, and free from unnecessary rules.

If you share the same spirit, whether you're an artist, a collaborator, or just someone who loves what we do,
you're already part of it.

Here, even the "i" stays small, because the music is bigger than the ego.
We're building this small corner of sound together, one release at a time, and there's always room for ideas, voices, and energy that match our vision.


Unsync isn't just a label. It's a space where music talks louder than rules, where independence matters more than approval, and where your sound belongs to you, not any one else.


Your energy, your sound, your vision..
If it clicks with ours, we're all ears and ready to collaborate.

If you did, thank you for having read what we had to say.
