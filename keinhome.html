KEIN NAME is an artist based in Central Europe, with a passion for producing and DJing Techno Music. KEIN NAME has been deeply immersed in the electronic music scene, carving out a unique space with a distinctive sound and style.

"I've been producing Music for several years now, over eight years actually, and I'm also a lot of other things.. such as a DJ, Designer, Photo and Videographer, Writer, and Creative Director. (but the list is somewhat longer) Despite my disability, I've never let it hold me back. Everything I've wanted to achieve, I've found my own way to do it. While others might follow a more conventional path, I've had to innovate and adapt, doing things differently but reaching the same heights.

And I think that is so great because now I can be a one-man company, since I'm able to do everything myself and don't need anybody to do what I truly want to. That's why I think it's safe to say that I'm actually starting from the bottom all alone, and with nothing.

BUT I could never forget the only two people that have actually helped me, and still do to this day.. Those people are my loving Mother and my Step Father.

Although I'm not a trust-fund baby, and neither are we rich or anything like that, my parents have helped me just like rich parents would, but with a hell of a lot more waiting involved.
For example:
if some rich kid wants a new computer, he probably gets it immediately. But if I need a new one, I also get it.. although, I do have to wait a year.

My Music and Sets are heavily inspired by the Old School Techno scene from the 90s to around 2010. I love those classic Tracks and that's what you'll hear when I play. They bring a certain Energy and Nostalgia that I just can't get enough of.
For me, it's all about staying true to that sound and keeping the spirit of those early days alive in most of what I do.

So, welcome to my world. It's not corporate, it's just me, making Music and spinning tracks from my living room."
